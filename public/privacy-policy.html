Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Capital Bridge Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI loan pre-qualification service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Property Details: Address, property type, purchase price, loan amount
  • Financial Information: Income, assets, credit history (only if you choose to provide)
  • Conversation Data: Messages exchanged with our AI chat agent
  • Voice Data: Audio recordings if you use voice input (transcribed and processed, not stored)

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited
  • Cookies: Session cookies for functionality, analytics cookies for improving service

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Pre-qualify you for loan products and provide preliminary offers
  • Contact you about your loan application via phone, email, or SMS
  • Connect you with our lending partners (with your explicit consent)
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 We Share With:

  • Lending Partners: Only with your explicit consent to process your loan application
  • Service Providers: Database hosting (Supabase), AI services (OpenAI, Perplexity), analytics
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights

4.2 We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data for marketing purposes without consent
  • Use your voice recordings beyond transcription for chat functionality

5. Voice Data Processing

When you use voice input:

  • Audio is recorded temporarily in your browser
  • Audio is sent to OpenAI Whisper API for transcription
  • Transcribed text is used for conversation; audio is immediately discarded
  • AI responses can be spoken using text-to-speech (optional)
  • Voice data is NOT stored on our servers

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage (Supabase with row-level security)
  • Secure API authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates

7. Data Retention

  • Lead Data: Retained for up to 7 years for compliance purposes
  • Chat History: Retained for 2 years unless deletion requested
  • Voice Recordings: Not retained (processed and discarded)
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained indefinitely

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications (reply STOP to SMS)
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent for data processing at any time

9. TCPA Compliance (Telephone Consumer Protection Act)

By providing your phone number, you expressly consent to receive:

  • Phone calls and text messages from Capital Bridge Solutions
  • Communications about your loan application, preliminary offers, and related services
  • Calls made using an automatic telephone dialing system or artificial/prerecorded voice

You may opt-out at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any text message
  • Emailing privacy@capitalbridgesolutions.com with "Do Not Call" in subject
  • Calling (949) 339-3555 to request removal

Standard message and data rates may apply.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Session management, security
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics (anonymized IP)
  • Advertising Cookies: Google Ads conversion tracking

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

11. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: AI chat and voice processing (Privacy Policy)
  • Perplexity AI: Property research (Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase: Database hosting (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (Privacy Policy)

12. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or shared
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

14. International Users

Our services are based in the United States. If you access our service from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S.
