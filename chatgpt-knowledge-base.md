## DSCR Loan Fundamentals

### What is a DSCR Loan?
A **Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) loan** is an investment property mortgage that qualifies you based on the property's cash flow instead of your personal W-2 income. This makes it ideal for:
- Self-employed investors
- High-net-worth individuals with complex tax situations
- Anyone who wants to avoid traditional income documentation
- Portfolio scaling (multiple properties)

### How DSCR is Calculated
**DSCR = Net Operating Income (NOI) √∑ Total Debt Service (PITIA)**

**Components:**
- **NOI:** Monthly rental income (actual or market rent from appraisal)
- **PITIA:** Principal + Interest + Taxes + Insurance + Association fees

**Example:**
- Monthly Rent: $4,000
- Monthly PITIA: $3,000
- DSCR = $4,000 √∑ $3,000 = **1.33** (positive cash flow)

### DSCR Values & What They Mean

| DSCR Value | Meaning | Lender View |
|------------|---------|-------------|
| **1.25+** | Strong positive cash flow | Excellent - best rates |
| **1.0-1.24** | Break-even to slight positive | Good - standard approval |
| **0.75-0.99** | Slight negative cash flow | Acceptable with compensating factors |
| **Below 0.75** | Significant negative | Difficult, may need adjustments |

### Minimum & Recommended DSCR Values

- **Standard:** 1.0 DSCR (break-even)

## Understanding DSCR - Simple Explanations

### "Explain Like I'm Five" - What is DSCR?

**The Lemonade Stand Analogy:**
Imagine you want to borrow money to buy a lemonade stand.

- The bank asks: **"Can this lemonade stand make enough money to pay me back?"**
- They DON'T ask about your allowance or how much your parents make
- They ONLY care: **Does the stand make more than the loan payment?**

**That's DSCR!**
- If the stand makes **$100/month** and the payment is **$80/month**, your DSCR is **1.25** (good!)
- If the stand makes **$80/month** and the payment is **$80/month**, your DSCR is **1.0** (break-even)
- If the stand makes **$60/month** and the payment is **$80/month**, your DSCR is **0.75** (losing money)

**For rental properties:**
- Replace "lemonade stand" with "rental house"
- Replace "lemonade sales" with "monthly rent"
- Replace "payment" with "mortgage + taxes + insurance"

### Key Concepts Made Simple

**Why DSCR Loans Exist:**
Traditional banks want to know about YOUR job and income. But what if:
- You're self-employed and write off everything?
- You have rental income from 10 properties but it "looks" small on tax returns?
- You don't want to show 2 years of tax returns?

**DSCR loans say:** "We don't care about YOUR money. We care about the PROPERTY's money."

**The Magic Formula:**
```
DSCR = Money Coming In √∑ Money Going Out

Money In = Monthly Rent
Money Out = Mortgage + Taxes + Insurance + HOA

DSCR > 1.0 = Property makes money ‚úÖ
DSCR = 1.0 = Property breaks even ‚ö†Ô∏è
DSCR < 1.0 = Property loses money ‚ùå
```
